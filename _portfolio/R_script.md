This script is an introduction to data analysis with R with a focus on data in the social sciences, particularly survey research. It explains the basic concepts of R as well as all necessary steps in analyzing data in R. It covers the data preparation as well as descriptive and multivariate statistics. Furthermore, it discusses specific topics such as graphs, indexing, and weighting in survey research.
